My favorite superheroes!

The Hulk

The Hulk is very strong. He's a normal guy but turns green when he's angry.

Iron Man

Iron Man can shoot blasts from his hands. He's really strong like the Hulk! He has a suit of armor to protect him. He has a cool mask to protect his face.

Captain America

Captain America has a shield that acts like a boomarang. He has a super cool cape and is very strong.

SpiderMan

SpiderMan is very strong! He can climb up walls and shoot strings from his hands to swing!
