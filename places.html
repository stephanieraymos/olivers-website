My favorite places!

Old Sacramento

My mommy's take me to Old Sacramento all the time. We usually start in DOCO and go to Estelle Bakery to get macarons and other yummy things. Then we walk down to Old Sac and walk around together.

Oak Creek Bmx

This track was where I had my very first race. I met a lot of cool kids my age and had so much fun racing them. I won 2 medals and a trophy at Oak Creek in the 3 balance bike moto!

Fair Oaks Bike Park

Fair Oaks Bike Park is my happy place. The bumps aren't as big as they are at Oak Creek so I can go super fast without being afraid.

Vava's house

Every Tuesday we drive to Fairfield to visit my vava. I love playing in the dirt in her backyard and eating all the sweets she gets for me. I love it when my cousin Killian is there so we can play together!
