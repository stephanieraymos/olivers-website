My favorite foods!

Bananas

Bananas are my favorite fruit because they're so yummy. They're easy to grab when you need a quick snack and have a lot of potassium which helps my muscles work properly!

Oranges

Oranges are so juicy and full of vitamin C! Vitamin C helps me stay healthy and keeps my immune system in tip top shape!

Avocado

Avocado's are so tasty!They actually contain more potassium than bananas and they're loaded with fiber which helps me go to the bathroom.

Smoothies

Smoothies are the best treat! My mom puts so many healthy things inside. My favorite is when she makes peanut butter banana smoothies! So yummy!
